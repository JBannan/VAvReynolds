NexusUni Case Bank

You have been given a list of cases specific to your trial to help you complete Trial Prep assignments. These cases are all accessible directly via Nexis Uni:

At least three of the cases in this bank will be useful to your side of the trial, regardless of whether you are representing the plaintiff or the defendant. Read the cases carefully! Some may be helpful to both plaintiff and defendant (in different ways). Be aware that both prosecution and defense are sharing this bank of cases. You will need to examine the cases that are most helpful to your argument, but you should also be familiar with the cases that are most helpful to your opponents' arguments and be prepared to respond to them. Also, use the skills you learned in the legal research assignment to ensure that your cases are both properly shepardized and are binding precedent in Virginia. Some cases may have been overturned or be otherwise unhelpful. THE CASES YOU SELECT ARE NOT BINDING PRECEDENT UNLESS THEY ARE FROM A VIRGINIA APPELLATE COURT OR A FEDERAL COURT THAT HAS JURISDICTION OVER VIRGINIA.

Witness List

Plaintiff witness: Attorney General of the Commonwealth of Virginia - Reynolds sent over 12,000 unsolicited bulk emails (SPAM) to users of CauliflowerOnline’s email service on 4 separate occasions.

Plaintiff witness: CauliflowerOnline Representative - Emails advertising the GoodIntentions charity were transmitted through the company's servers located in Fairfax, Virginia. The emails caused a noticeable burden on the servers, though CauliflowerOnline received very few complaints about the emails from users.

Defendant witness: Jeremy Reynolds - Reynolds sent emails to users of the CauliflowerOnline email service. These emails advertised the GoodIntentions charity and its mission. This advertisement also mentioned the retail stores and merchandise available for purchase that would go towards the charity’s mission. These emails also gave the recipient the option to opt out of receiving future emails.

Defendant witness: GoodIntentions charity Representative - Mailing lists for CauliflowerOnline were obtained via a business partner along with other email service providers. Promotional materials were distributed to these mailing lists on behalf of the organization, a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
